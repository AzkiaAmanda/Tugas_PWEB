Catalog

Standard

A comfortable room with basic amenities, ideal for budget travelers. Equipped with a standard-size bed, private bathroom, and basic amenities such as TV and Wi-Fi.

Single Room

A room exclusively for one guest, usually equipped with one single bed. Suitable for solo travelers seeking privacy and comfort.

Twin Room

A room with two single beds, suitable for travel companions or small families. Provides separate sleeping spaces for extra convenience.

Deluxe Room

A spacious room with enhanced and elegant facilities. Usually includes a queen or king-size bed, scenic views, and premium amenities such as a minibar and luxury toiletries.

Suite

A luxurious room with a separate living area, often equipped with a kitchenette. Perfect for guests seeking an exclusive experience or a long-term stay.

Presidential Suite

The most luxurious option with an exceptionally spacious area, exclusive design, and personalized services. Includes premium facilities such as a dining area, jacuzzi, and butler service.
